How to Provide Effective Employee Recognition?

Employee recognition is a communication tool that reinforces and rewards the most important outcomes that people create for your business.

Call to Action!

If you ever get caught sleeping at work just slowly raise your hand and say "In Jesus' name I pray"

No Exceptions

You need to make all employees eligible for the recognition. You should never exclude any employee or group of employees.

More Info

Well defined criteria

The more clearly you design and communicate the criteria for eligibility for the award, the easier it is for employees to perform accordingly.

More Info

Encourage people

The recognition should occur as close to the performance of the actions as possible, so the recognition reinforces the behavior the employer wants to encourage.
