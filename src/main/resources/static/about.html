What actually is Colleague Recognition Tool ?

From development point of view it is a standard Web Application that you can access from your browser. It can be integrated within the organization's network and could be accessed only in organization's network. The whole idea is based on the fact that people need to be encouraged if they are doing their work correctly. The tool is used to vote for a person whenever an employee wants. Different categories could be selected when voting for employee(work related or not). The winner could be given some kind of incentive or just a small present. A future idea might be the opposite implementation - people giving negative votes for a colleague like - lazy person, not doing his job.. etc. If you think you like the idea please contact us for a free consulting session.

If you really like the product we can send you a consultant who can install, integrate and bootstrap the application in your work environment. The data entered in is absolutely private will not be used for any kind of further processing.
